Privacy Policy

Voice Data: When you use our speaking practice features, we may collect and process audio recordings of your voice for the purposes of providing feedback and assessment.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our application, including interactions, learning patterns, progress statistics, and feature usage.

Technical Data: We automatically collect device information, operating system, browser type, and IP address when you access our platform.
