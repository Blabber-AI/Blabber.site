Your go-to resource for getting the most out of our AI-Powered Language Tutor

Getting Started

Contact us to start the integration process. We'll collaborate with your school to define goals, tailor the app to your needs, and train teachers for a seamless setup.

Blabber is compatible with smartphones and tablets. Students and teachers each use a unique app interface designed for their roles.

Currently, we offer English as the target language, with fully customizable learning plans for schools.

For Schools & Teachers

Blabber works closely with schools to fully align the app with the Education Ministry's curriculum. From setting goals to tailoring exercises and activities, we ensure the app supports your school's specific requirements.

Teachers access a dedicated interface to track progress, assign tasks, and adapt lessons to students' needs. Training is provided to ensure smooth implementation.

Yes. Teachers can create and manage multiple class groups within the app, ensuring easy organization and flexibility.

For Students

Students use the app to practice speaking, listening, and vocabulary in a fun, interactive way. Feedback on grammar and pronunciation is provided instantly for continuous improvement.

Blabber creates a safe, private environment where students can practice as much as they need, with no fear of judgment.

Students receive regular progress updates and can review session highlights directly in the app.

Payments & Pricing

Payments are processed through the "Gefen" system on a per-student basis. A minimum number of students is required for each school.

Pricing is designed to be scalable, ensuring fair rates for schools of all sizes. Contact us for details.

Troubleshooting & Technical Issues

First, try restarting your device and ensuring permissions for the app (microphone, camera, etc.) are enabled. If the issue persists, contact our support team for quick assistance.

Ensure your internet connection is stable and check device settings. For additional help, refer to our troubleshooting guide or reach out to support.

Privacy & Data Protection

All data is encrypted and securely stored. We never share personal information without explicit consent.

Report any issues directly through the app or email us at contact@blabber.site. All reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly.
