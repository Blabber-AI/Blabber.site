How Blabber Works in Schools

Our AI-powered language tutor integrates with your school curriculum to provide personalized learning experiences that support educators and engage students.

1

Integrate with Your Curriculum

We work with your school to align Blabber with existing curriculum standards, ensuring seamless integration with your language department's goals and methodologies.

2

Empower Your Students

Students engage in real-time conversations with our AI avatar. They practice pronunciation, discuss curriculum topics, and build confidence in a safe, judgment-free environment.

3

Track Classroom Progress

Teachers receive detailed analytics and insights on student performance. Our system identifies areas for improvement, helps educators target instruction, and provides data to demonstrate learning outcomes.
