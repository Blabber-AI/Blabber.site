How Blabber Works

Our AI tutor seamlessly integrates with your curriculum, providing personalized learning that supports educators and engages students.

Craft the perfect lesson

  • Blabber's content is aligned with Israel Ministry of Education's (MoE) standards, textbooks and COBE requirements.
  • Choose from a variety of lessons tailored to every grade level.
  • Customize the lesson to suit your vision.

Let them speak!

Blabber delivers the lesson to each student individually or to all of them together in class. All by an AI Private Tutor, which promises a safe and fun space to practice and learn from mistakes.

Grow with your students

  • Blabber provides top-tier analysis over each student, based on MoE's official metrics.
  • See how students did on a single task, and over the course of time.
  • Blabber also adapts to each student's abilities and needs as they progress - just like you do!
