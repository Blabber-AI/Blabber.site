Blabber Support Center

Your go-to resource for getting the most out of our AI-Powered Language Tutor

1. Getting Started

After you sign up, you can choose a target language, specify your goals, and schedule a “Zoom-like” session with our AI avatar. You’ll practice speaking and listening in real time, learn new vocabulary, and receive immediate feedback on grammar and pronunciation. Each session is tailored to your proficiency level and curriculum needs.

Log in and navigate to the “Schedule Session” tab. Select a date and time, add any lesson materials or special requests, and confirm. You’ll receive an email or in-app notification once your session is scheduled.

Blabber runs on most modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and devices (PC, Mac, iOS, Android). For optimal performance, ensure your browser is up to date, and check that your device meets our minimum system requirements.

2. For Teachers & Schools

3. For Students

Our avatar-led sessions provide a private, judgment-free environment. You can repeat words and phrases as often as you like. The AI will gently correct mistakes and gradually introduce new vocabulary based on your comfort level.

We respect your privacy, so we only store session transcripts (text versions) unless you opt in to record audio or video. If you opt in, your session video/audio is available for a limited time in your account under “Past Sessions.”

4. Payments & Pricing

We accept major credit/debit cards via Stripe. All payments are securely processed, and no financial information is ever shared with third parties.

Schools can set up a single billing account and purchase session packages at discounted rates. Simply contact our sales team for customized institution-wide plans.

5. Troubleshooting & Technical Issues

First, check your browser or device settings to ensure microphone access is granted. If the problem persists, restart your device and try again. For further assistance, consult our Audio Troubleshooting Guide or reach out to Support.

A strong, stable internet connection is crucial. Try moving closer to your Wi-Fi router or using a wired connection. Close unnecessary applications that may consume bandwidth. If issues persist, contact your internet provider or our Support team for further guidance.

6. Privacy & Data Protection

We use encryption to keep your data secure, and we never share personal details such as email addresses or phone numbers without your explicit consent. Please review our Privacy Policy for more information.

If you encounter any form of harassment or inappropriate content, please report it immediately through the in-app reporting feature or email us at contact@blabber.site. We take these matters seriously and will investigate promptly.
